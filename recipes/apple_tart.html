Apple Tart

This recipe is based on the youtube channel J'adore자도르's recipe from the video [Eng Sub]사과 타르트(애플파이) 만들기 How to make French Apple tart, pie ｜자도르

Ingredients
  • Tart Shell
    • 136g cake flour (22.66g corn starch, 115.6g all-purpose flour)
    • 70g cold butter
    • 32g cold water
    • 12g cold egg yolk (1 egg yolk or so)
    • 1 tsp or so salt
  • Flour Cream (original recipe uses almond cream)
    • 80g room temperature butter
    • 80g room temperature egg
    • 80g sugar
      • original recipe asks for sugar powder, sugar worked fine
    • 63.3g all-purpose flour
      • original recipe asks for 80g almond flour, 10g cake flour
    • original recipe also uses 5g rum (gold rum apparently goes well with apple?)
  • Topping
    • 6 apples(?)
    • 40g apricot jam
    • 10g water
Instructions
  1. Tart Shell
    1. Cube the butter.
    2. In a food processor, put the flour and butter and pulse till the butter becomes rice or pea sized pieces.
    3. Empty the contents of the food processor onto a worksurface and make a divot in the flour.
    4. Pour your egg yolk, water, salt mixture into the divot.
    5. Mix the mixture with the flour within the divot till it has been mostly absorbed (making sure to keep the mixture cold so the butter doesn't melt).
    6. Then roughly combine the flour till it becomes a single mass without any powder.
    7. You can then cut the mass in half and places the two halves over each other and press them together to create layers.
      • The result was still quite nice even without this step.
    8. Wrap and keep in fridge for around 1 hour.
    9. roll out and line your tart pan
    10. Place back in the fridge till ready for use.
  2. Flour Cream
    1. Cream the butter.
    2. Put in the sugar and whisk till just incorporated (the goal is not to add air)
    3. Whisk the egg in slowly to the mixture, adding in parts (around 8 parts).
      • the 1 to 1 ratio of egg to butter can lead to easy separation if the egg is too cold or the egg is not added gradually.
    4. Sift in the flour (or the almond flour and cake flour)
    5. Lightly fold in the flour.
    6. (optional) Add the rum and lightly mix.
  3. Topping
    1. Fill the lined tart pan with the cream.
    2. Make sure the cream layer is level.
    3. Cut the apples into thin slices (I kept the skin for looks).
    4. Place the apples on top of the cream in a spiral pattern.
    5. Do more than one layer.
    6. Place in a 170°C (338°F) oven for around 50-55 minutes.
    7. When done, remove from oven and brush a warm mixture of the apricot jam with the water.
    8. Then, when the tart has cooled slightly, repeat this process of brushing the jam mixture.
  4. Extra: Apricot Jam
    1. Peel and core your apricots and place the flesh in a pot with an approximately equal amount of sugar.
    2. Add a bit of lemon juice and cook till it becomes thick and glossy.
    3. Turn the heat off and add a bit more lemon juice.
