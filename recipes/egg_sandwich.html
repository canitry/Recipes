Egg Sandwich

Ingredients
  • 3 boiled eggs
  • 1 spoonful of mayonnaise
  • 1/8 diced red onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 slices of bread
  • butter
  • A handful of your leafy greenery of choice
Instructions
  1. Mash the peeled eggs.
  2. Add the onion, mayonnaise, and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Spoon the egg mixture onto one slice of bread.
  5. Place the greens on top of the egg.
  6. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter side down.
  7. Wrap the sandwich in parchment paper and cut in half.
