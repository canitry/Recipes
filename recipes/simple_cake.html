Simple Cake (Genoise)

You can put whatever you want inside the cake as well (so long as it's not too moist). I think I put jelly beans in this one (sacrilege).

This recipe is from the youtube channel Cooking Tree from the video 폭신폭신 제누와즈 만들기, 기본 스펀지 케이크 시트 : Basic Sponge cake sheet Recipe, Genoise : ケーキシート -Cookingtree쿠킹트리.

Ingredients
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 45ml milk
  • 70g melted butter
  • 4 egg whites
  • 100g sugar
  • 110g cake flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
Instructions
  1. Whisk your egg yolks till it becomes liquid, add the milk and melted butter and mix
  2. In a different bowl, whip your egg whites, when big bubbles form, add the sugar in 3 parts till it becomes a meringue.
  3. Sift your dry ingredients into the meringue and fold (we're trying to prevent air loss).
  4. Carefully fold in the egg yolk mixture into the meringue.
    • I recommend mixing a bit of the egg white mixture into the egg yolk mixture then putting the resulting mixture into the egg white mixture to fold.
  5. Pour the batter into your pan (line with parchment or butter) and hit the bottom of the pan lightly against the table to get rid of any air bubbles.
  6. Bake in a 180°C (°F) oven for 35-40 minutes (check with a toothpick).
