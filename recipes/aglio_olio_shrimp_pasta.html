Aglio Olio Shrimp Pasta

This recipe is based off of a 우리의식탁 recipe: 새우 오일 파스타

Ingredients
  • 2 servings of spaghetti (around one third of a Garofalo Spaghetti bag)
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 8 Tiger shrimps
  • 1 dried pepper (pepperocino)
    • The original recipe asks for 3
  • 3 tablespoons or so of olive oil/cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon or so of Better Than Bouillon chicken stock
  • A pat of butter
  • A spoonful of basil pesto
  • grated Parmesan
  • salt
  • pepper
Instructions
  1. If the shrimp is frozen, place in the refrigerator to defrost a few hours prior
  2. Run the shrimp under cold water a few times then peel the shrimp, saving the shell.
  3. Slice the garlic into thin slices, around a millimeter in thickness.
  4. Generously salt a pot of water (the amount of salt two fingers and a thumb will pick up) and place it on the stove on high heat till it boils.
  5. When it boils, place the pasta in the pot and cook for 11 minutes (or how you generally cook pasta).
  6. When the pasta is done, strain the pasta so it doesn't bloat
    • If you would like to use the pasta water in the sauce, keep the pasta water
  7. Place a pan on heat.
  8. Once it heats up, put in oil.
  9. Lower the heat and add the garlic and pepper to the pan, stir, making sure they do not burn.
  10. When the garlic has browned, add the shrimp shells
  11. When the shrimp shells have reddened, add the shrimp, along with salt, pepper, and your pat of butter.
  12. When the shrimp is thoroughly cooked, remove the shrimp and set off to the side, and add some water (preferrably pasta water) with the chicken stock dissolved into it
    • You may also want to remove the pepper
  13. Reduce the water in the pan till it becomes a saucy consistency, when reduced, remove the shells and add the shrimp back in
    • If you want the sauce to become richer, you could add more water and wait till reduced again
  14. Add the pasta into the sauce and add a spoonful of pesto, toss until incorporated.
  15. Plate your finished pasta and garnish with grated Parmesan
