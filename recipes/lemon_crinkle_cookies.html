Ingredients
  • 90g (unsalted) butter (using salted didn't cause any obvious problems)
  • 110g sugar
  • 20g egg (ie. 1 large egg or so)
  • 30g starch syrup (ë¬¼ì—¿, corn syrup works too)
  • 2g vanilla extract
  • 10g lemon juice
  • lemon zest
  • 170g all-purpose flour
  • 3g baking powder
  • 3g baking soda
Instructions
  1. Cream the butter until soft
  2. Mix in the sugar with the butter until incorportated
  3. Mix in the egg, vanilla extract, and starch syrup
  4. Mix in the lemon juice and lemon zest
  5. Sift in the dry ingredients into the butter mixture and fold until it just comes together
  6. Shape into spheres and roll in sugar then place on a lined baking tray and press down into coins
  7. Bake in a 180°C (356°F) oven for around 9 minutes
    • My cookies took a lot longer than 9 minutes, I would recommend checking till they've spread and cracked, you can smell the cookies, and they've slightly browned on the outer edges.
